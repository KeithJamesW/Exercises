1. Var is both reassignable and you can redeclare it, but let cannot be redeclared, only reassigned. Hoisting
is permitted with Var and not let.

2. Var is both reassignable and can be redeclared, but const can not be either reassigned or redeclared.

3. Let can at least be reassigned, but const cannot be reassigned or recdeclared. 


4. Hoisting is where the variables are put before their first use if they weren't already. This can 
only be done with var.
